Car Parking Tracker

The car parking tracker provides real-time data on the availability of parking spaces in the DCU campus.

The data is collected using sensors installed in the parking lots, which monitor the occupancy of each space.

The sensors can detect the presence or absence of a vehicle in each parking slot.
